## Description

A different approach is taken for fabric defect detection. In reality, positive samples, that is normal fabric images without defects, are easy to obtain, while negative samples are difficult to get. So taking advantage of positive samples should be a viable way. In this project, Gabor filters are used for feature extraction, then it is followed by a dimensionality reduction technique, and lastly, the Nearest Neighbor density estimation used for classification.

## What learned

1. Gabor filter is good to extract textures
2. Study Auto Encoder and its variants for dimensionality reduction (Stacked AE, Variational AE, etc)
3. Nearest Neighbor density estimation and Parzen Window for classification.
