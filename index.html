Hao Zhou

I will join the Department of Computer Science at University of Texas at Dallas as a tenure-track Assistant Professor in Fall 2026. My research focuses on developing AI-powered wearable systems that sense and understand human behavior and health.

I received my Ph.D. from Penn State, where my research was recognized with several awards, including the Penn State Alumni Association Dissertation Award and the Best Paper Award for Edge IoT AI at IoTDI 2023. I was also selected for the ACM MobiSys Rising Star Forum. I have gained industry research experience at Samsung Research America and Microsoft Research Asia.
