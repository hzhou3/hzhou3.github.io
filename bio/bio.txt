Hao Zhou received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from The Pennsylvania State University. His research focuses on AI-powered mobile and wearable systems, time-series representation learning, and low-cost sensing for healthcare and accessibility. His work has been recognized with several honors, including a Best Paper Award and the Penn State Alumni Association Dissertation Award. He is currently with Digital Health Lab at Samsung Research America, where he works on foundation models for multimodal sensory data.
